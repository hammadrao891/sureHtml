SurePeople cares about and is committed to protect the privacy of its users.

Last revised on November 12, 2020

SurePeople privacy notice

SurePeople permits you (”you”, ”User”, ”Public User”, or ”Registered User”) to browse the SurePeople Website, take the Prism® psychometric assessment for your own use or to be used by one of our client companies, who may be employers or consultants (each a ”Client”) and offers you, as an individual and/or these Clients access to certain data, tools and services accessible through the Site, including, but not limited to, psychometric assessments, 360 assessments, surveys, learning/development action plans, content and other services (the ”Service”).

Below we explain how SurePeople collects, uses, discloses, safeguards and provides accessibility to your information collected from the SurePeople Website as a Public User or Registered User.

Personal Information Collected
  • First and Last Name
  • Email Address

Purpose

This personal information is collected and used to respond to your inquiry and/or process and complete the transactions you requested.

Personal Information Collected
  • Internet Protocol (IP) Address
  • Device ID
  • Browser Type

This anonymous information is collected to improve Website functionality, and tailor more relevant content or services for you.

Registered User

As a Registered User of SurePeople, we will collect, score, maintain and update the results of any assessment, survey, program activity, or other features in your profile, as well as all the information we have received about and from you. Your information, excluding surveys labeled anonymous, will be disclosed to the Clients (and any others) you designate. The following personal information is used to create your SurePeople profile:

Personal Information Collected
  • First and Last Name
  • Title
  • Email Address

  • Phone Number (Optional)
Purpose

This information is collected and used to build out and provide you access to services, and to respond to your requests.

This information will not be shared with any other entity.

How and why we use cookies

Registered User

When you visit www.SurePeople.com and move from page to page, read pages, or download content onto your computer or device, we may collect certain information, including, without limitation, which pages are visited, what is downloaded, the domains our visitors came from (e.g. "facebook.com” or "google.com") and the addresses of sites visited immediately before coming to our site. SurePeople may also collect certain information through the use of "cookies." Cookies are bits of electronic information that a Website can transfer to a visitor's computer or device to help tailor and keep records of his or her visit to the Website.

Cookies help SurePeople improve the site's content by enhancing your overall experience on our Website. SurePeople does not store any personally identifiable information in these cookies. SurePeople uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to help us analyze how the Website is used. The information generated by the cookie about use of the Website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers owned by Google.

Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. The collected data is not tied to any single individual, no individual profiles are created, and Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your Web browser; however, please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this Website.

How we use personal information

Registered User

First and foremost, SurePeople does not sell or rent your personal information.

In addition to processing transactions, responding to requests, and providing Assessment Information to Clients and/or other persons or entities you designate, SurePeople may use anonymous, aggregate statistics about our Users, traffic patterns, and related information for exploring business development, advertising, and marketing opportunities and for educational purposes.

Moreover, SurePeople may use de-identified information including Assessment Information for analytical and statistical purposes, as well as to present anonymous profiles of candidates to prospective employers who we believe are looking for particular capabilities. In the event a particular Client is interested in learning your identity, we will seek your explicit consent before disclosing any personally identifiable information about you to that Client.

By using our Website, you consent to the collection and use of your information by SurePeople as stated above. If we decide to change our Privacy Notice, we will post changes on this page and/or make other commercially reasonable efforts to make you aware of the changes made.

We provide information to service providers under contract who help with our business operations. We disclose information to law enforcement, governmental agencies, or authorized third parties, in response to a verified request relating to a criminal investigation or alleged illegal activity or any other activity that may expose us, you, or any other User to legal liability. In such events, we will disclose information that we believe to be relevant and necessary to the investigation or inquiry. Such information will be disclosed in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Additionally, we may disclose personal information in situations that we believe to be emergencies involving potential threats to the physical safety or any person or property if we believe that the information in any way relates to that threat. In the event we are engaged in or contemplating a merger or other corporate acquisition with another business entity, personal information may be disclosed to and would likely be acquired by the other business entity. Such disclosure, if any, would be covered by an appropriate confidentiality agreement.

How we secure personal information

SurePeople is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. While we have implemented reasonable security measures to safeguard personal information, please keep in mind that "perfect security" does not exist on the Internet.

How you can review or request changes to your personal information

Registered User

You may review the personal information we have collected regarding you at any time by accessing your SurePeople profile settings. If you need to make any changes or corrections to your personal information please do so here. If you are unable to do this yourself, please contact us at privacy@surepeople.com and describe specifically what needs to be changed, up to deletion of your SurePeople profile. We will try to complete your request as promptly and reasonably possible as permitted under applicable law. We reserve the right to request documentation from you to support any changes to the information.

In some circumstances, SurePeople collects information under the direction of its Clients and therefore has no direct relationship with the Registered Users whose personal information it processes on behalf of its Clients. A Registered User who seeks access, or who seeks to correct, amend, or delete inaccurate personal data should direct their query to the data owner (i.e., SurePeople’s Client). If the Client requests that SurePeople remove this personal data, SurePeople will respond to their request within 30 business days of its receipt of such request.

Public User

Upon request, SurePeople will make a reasonable effort to provide you whether we hold any of your personal information within reasonable time. Users may request updates, deletion, access, or changes to their personal information by contacting us at privacy@surepeople.com or by call us at 1-855-755-SURE (7873). We will respond to any requests for access to personal information within 30 days of receipt of such request.

Aditional CCPA information for California residents

SurePeople does not sell personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration.

Under the California Consumer Protection Act (”CCPA”), California residents are granted five consumer rights that SurePeople will uphold:

  1. The right to know which personal information is being collected
  2. The right to know if personal data is being sold or shared, and to whom
  3. The right to object to the sale of personal data
  4. The right to access one’s own personal information
  5. The right to equal service and price, even for consumers who exercise their privacy rights

    Any California Resident wishing to exercise their right to access personal information (held by SurePeople) must submit a verifiable consumer request to SurePeople by either:

  6. Emailing SurePeople at privacy@surepeople.com
  7. Calling SurePeople at 1-855-755-SURE (7873)

Please note that only an individual who is a resident of California (or the legal guardian of such individual in the case of a minor child) may make a request under the CCPA.
